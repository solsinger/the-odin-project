Before I barrage you, I'd just like to point out these are the same 12 or so ingredients in different combos.

This is just so you can stop thinking and start doing!

These recipes are intended to serve 1 person.

Method

  1. Cut onion into small pieces - whatever size YOU like.
  2. Pre-heat the oil in your skillet or wok once you're done slicing onions.
  3. Quarter or slice your veg. Let's face it, char is fucking delicious.
  4. Stir onions every half minute as you cut up the veg.
  5. Once onions begin to caramelize (get brown), toss in your veg! (~3 minutes.)
  6. STIR. Stir and watch until veg begins to brown. (~2-4 minutes.)
  7. Add any meat you have that is pre-cooked so it reheats with your meal (~1-2 minutes).
  8. Stir around until there's enough char to make your soul happy!
  9. Put in a vessel and top with salt to your liking (5 turns of a Himalayan salt mill, for instance.)
Tips:
  • Variegate the way you cut up the vegetables for different experiences.
  • Always cut up larger bits of veg in order to ensure even cooking.
  • Always pre-heat your oil in the pan for 2 minutes on 6 or 7 (medium high heat, basically).
  • Pre-cook any meat additions and add them in the last minute.
  • Cover the bottom of the skillet in oil. :)
