Tips:
  • Variegate the way you cut up the vegetables for different experiences.
  • Always cut up larger bits of veg in order to ensure even cooking.
  • Always pre-heat your oil in the pan for 2 minutes on 5 or 6 (medium heat, basically).
  • Pre-cook any meat additions and add them in the last minute.

Crystol's Stir Fry #1: Broccoli & Onion

Ingredients

  • 1 small head of broccoli (1/2 larger size)
  • 1 onion
  • 1/4 cup cooked meat (OPTIONAL)

Method

  • Cut onion into small pieces - whatever size YOU like.
  • Pre-heat the oil in your skillet or wok once you're done slicing onions.
  • Cut broccoli into florets, bisecting anything large or slicing like a mushroom. (Cooks faster.)
  • Once onions begin to caramelize (get brown), toss in your broccoli! (About 3 minutes.)
  • STIR. Stir and watch until broccoli begins to brown. (About 3 minutes.)
  • Add any meat you have that is pre-cooked so it reheats with your meal.
  • Stir around until there's enough char to make your soul happy!
  • Put in a vessel and top with salt to your liking (5 turns of a Himalayan salt mill, for instance.)
