Crystol's Pixxa

Ingredients

  • 1 Wegmans cauliflower pizza crust (frozen food section)
  • 3 TBS avocado oil
  • 1/2 bag of plant-based shredded "mozzarella" (avoid turmeric and coconut if possible)
  • Basil, to taste
  • Oregano, to taste
  • Garlic powder, to taste
  • Onion powder, to taste
  • 1/4 onion, diced (OPTIONAL)
  • 1/4 cup of minced meat, cooked (OPTIONAL)

Method

  1. Pre-heat oven at 410 Fahrenheit.
  2. Take crust out of the freezer to thaw.
  3. Put on baking sheet/pan and cover with oil. Be generous or your cheese won't spread.
  4. Cover the oil and crust in dustings of your preferred spices. (Use what you can eat, I can't eat jack.)
  5. Cover in half a bag of "cheese." (Dairy is the devil and key to leaky gut syndrome for this girl.)
  6. Add veg and pre-cooked meats until it's no longer boring -- I provided some ideas in case your brain is off.
  7. Put into oven once it's fully pre-heated.
  8. Set a timer for 15 minutes. (My oven is old, your oven might be better... KEEP AN EYE ON THAT THING!)
  9. Switch to BROIL for 1-2 minutes if you like extra crispy toppings.

The reason this is called PIXXA is because I found out that you can only call it PIZZA if there's tomato sauce.

That's right... there's no such thing as buffalo chicken pizza or white pizza. It's a myth!
