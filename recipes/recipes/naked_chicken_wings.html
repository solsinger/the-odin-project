Crystol's Naked Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • frozen wings, bone-in, parts or whole

Method

  1. Pre-heat oven at 400 Fahrenheit.
  2. Pull out a sheet pan.
  3. Line the sheet pan with wings.
  4. Wait for the DING to tell you it's pre-heated (or the light.)
  5. Bake for 25 minutes.
  6. Take sheet out of the oven. Flip everyfing over with tongs. Put back in oven.
  7. Bake for another 25 minutes.
  8. Leave in oven until you're ready to serve. (At least 5 minutes.)

Make It Yours:
  • Mix together a variety of seasonings to sprinkle over the wings. (Onion powder, garlic powder, basil, oregano are the ones I tolerate best these days, but you can use whatever you want.)
  • Mix half and half: coconut oil + your favorite hot sauce. [Warm the oil until melted, duh.]
  • Mix half and half: coconut oil + your favorite enchilada sauce. [Warm the oil until melted, duh.]
  • Sprinkle fake parmesan all over it with melted fake butter. You can add some herbses if you like, as well.
  • Serve it with a dip on the side. Maybe a cashew cream sort of based dip?
