Crystol's Cashew Cream

Ingredients

  • cashews, raw
  • mineral/spring water

Method

  • Boil some clean water.
  • Toss in six handfuls of raw cashews. (Roasted are crunchy, don't be silly here, mmkay?)
  • Boil for ten minutes or until cashews are soft.
  • Drain & rinse, allowing to cool and dry.
  • In a nice blender or food processor, throw these in with 1/6 cup of water and blend.
  • Add tiny amounts of water until a creamy consistency is achieved.
  • Add seasonings, spices, whatever, and store for consumption later.

Use a blender or food processor to combine any of the following. They'll probably taste better the day after. :)

Crystol's Avocado Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cashew cream
  • 2 avocados, peeled and all
  • garlic powder, to taste
  • onion powder, to taste
  • salt, to taste

Crystol's Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cashew cream
  • 2 TBS cocoa powder (OPTIONAL)
  • 1 TBS high-quality vanilla extract
  • monkfruit, to taste

Crystol's Ranch Alternative

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cashew cream
  • garlic powder, to taste
  • onion powder, to taste
  • basil, to taste
  • salt, to taste
  • water to desired consistency
