Crystol's Pumpkin Pancake Pie

Ingredients

  • 1/3 can of organic Wegmans pumpkin puree
  • 2 cups of pancake mix
  • 1 tbs vanilla
  • 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice extract
  • 1.5 cups "milk"

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Fahrenheit.
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients together.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry and mix thoroughly.
  4. Transfer into a 9x9 baking dish or pie plate (if you don't mix it inside your oven safe dish).
  5. Cook for 50 minutes.
