Crystol's Meatballs

Incidentally, this is a recipe tons of people know. Rename it and make it yours, buddy.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef/meat
  • 1 TSP salt
  • 1 TBS onion powder
  • 1 TBS garlic powder
  • 1 TBS parsley
  • 1 TSP oregano
  • 1 TSP basil

Method

  1. Preheat oven or air fryer to 400 F. Pull out a rimmed baking sheet if using the oven. (There will be fat to discard.)
  2. In a mixing bowl of adequate size, mix all the ingredients together. If using your hands, make sure you wash them well after.
  3. Use a meat baller (or melon baller) to create meatballs of exactly the same size effortlessly.
  4. Space the meatballs out on the baking sheet so that they don’t touch.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes.
  6. If using an air fryer, fry at 400 F for 10-12 minutes.

Flipping in the middle will produce more crispiness, of course.
