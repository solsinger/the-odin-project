Crystol's Masala Chai

Ingredients

  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 12 cardamom pods
  • 2 tsp peppercorns
  • 6 cloves
  • 2 in. ginger, sliced
  • 4 star anise
  • 6 allspice
  • 1 cup "sugar" (optional)
  • 4 cups water
  • 6 black tea bags
