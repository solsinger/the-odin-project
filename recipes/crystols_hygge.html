Hygge. Danish for being awesome.

Crystol's Hygge

  • Always wear soft, comfy clothing with a texture that makes you melt.
  • Put the softest sheets and blankets you can find on your bed (and couch, if applicable.)
  • COMFY PILLOWS. Need I say more?
  • Live life in pajamas, if you can! People suck, anyway, so put on your people clothes only when you must.
  • Get plenty of sleep.
  • Burn candles daily for at least 1 hour.
  • Clean a little every day, every step of the way.
  • Get plenty of water.
  • Make sure everything you eat makes your mouth sing. (Even if you burn it.)
  • Drink coffee AND tea. Life is short.
  • Meditate on how amazing it is to be alive.
  • Go outside and thank nature for existing.
  • Ruminate in gratitude.
  • Look for the silver lining... there is always a silver lining.
  • Kiss the chef!
